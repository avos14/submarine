How to play

  1. Select your ships from the left-hand side.
  2. Place your ships on the map called "Your Fleet" and click "Start Game" or click "Place Randomly and Start".
  3. Click on the cells of the enemy's map called "Enemy Fleet" to find and destroy all five enemy ships.
  4. The computer will fire on your ships immediately after you fire on its ships.

Special functions

  1. You can place your ships randomly or one-by-one.
  2. You can rotate the ship.(Click the ship, click Rotate Ship, place ship on map)
  3. you can choose which size of board you want to play on.
  4. You can cheat.
  5. You can save and load.
